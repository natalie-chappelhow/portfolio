The Designer

Monica Cole-Simonetta is a first year student studying Games Design at University of Central Lancashire.

She is a creative person who especially enjoys creating different games and the assests for them, often doing so in her spare time.

This website showcases two of the games that have been created by Monica, a platform game and a top down game.
