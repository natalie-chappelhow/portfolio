Platform Game

The aim of this game is to get Emilia, the chief fairy of Paichnídi to saftey over the poisonous spikes and past the horns of the beast whilst collecting as many coins as she can carry along the way.

To play the game you need to use the left and right hand mouse keys to move Emilia in different directions. The up arrow key is used to make her jump whist a combination of the up arrow key then the right or left arrow keys are used to make her jump over the poisonous spikes.

Keep an eye out for the money bags which offer more coins.
