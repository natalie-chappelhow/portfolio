Top Down Game

The aim of this game is to shoot as many of the birds as possible without being hit by them. To do this the turtle uses its special bird killing stones.

To play the game you need to use the arrow keys to move the turtle around the screen. To fire the special stones you need to use the left hand click of the mouse.

A counter is present on the screen so you can keep up to date with your score and compare with friends.

This is an addictive game so once you have been hit at, you instantly want to play again to better any previous scores.

Have fun trying to Burst a Bird!
